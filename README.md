## How to apply an Icon to an application
- 1: Right click on your application launcher and select "Edit Applications"
<img width="274" height="227" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0eff306a-957b-4204-9438-878e4523d5b0" />

 <p> </p>
 <p> </p>

- 2: Search for the application you want to change the Icon of.
<img width="259" height="124" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/311e66e4-cb93-47c0-8702-68e0fc917800" />

 <p></p>
 <p></p>

- 3: When you click on the application, a menu will open up on the right side. Click on the existing Icon and click "Browse"
<img width="1099" height="346" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/38426c8f-62ab-4b86-abec-20775283b759" />

 <p></p>
 <p></p>

- 4: Select the Icon you want to apply and click "Open"
<img width="964" height="566" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/971cc2bf-8a44-430c-b0c0-b111b7da1978" />

 <p></p>
 <p></p>

- 5: Last step: Simply press "Save" and repeat the process for all the icons you want to change
<img width="325" height="213" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e3ba64ff-0aa4-4240-afce-2fe5874f3eb0" />
